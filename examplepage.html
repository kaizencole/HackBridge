Chinese Fried Rice

Photo

Ingredients

    Exact Measures

  • 2 cups white rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup chopped baby carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cups water

    • According To Taste

  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil

Directions

  • Preparation

      Chop baby carrots into pea-sized bits. Rinse and wash the rice.

    • Step 1

        In a saucepan, combine rice and water. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.

    • Step 2

        Heat wok over high heat. Pour in oil, then stir in carrots and peas; cook about 30 seconds.

    • Step 3

        Crack in eggs, stirring quickly to scramble eggs with vegetables. Stir in cooked rice. Shake in soy sauce, and toss rice to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil, and toss again.
